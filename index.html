TOFU
EXPERIENCE

Tofu, or soybean curd, is a food made by coagulating soy milk and pressing the resulting curds into blocks. It is a versatile food, popular in many Asian cuisines and as a plant-based protein source, because it has a very mild flavor and can absorb the taste of other ingredients. Tofu is made by soaking and crushing soybeans, separating the milk from the pulp, and then adding a coagulant to form curds
